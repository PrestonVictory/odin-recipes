How to make a Smoothie

Smoothie time

Here is a smoothie, it looks so good. I eat smoothies every day. They are so good because you can put many different types of food in them and you can eat them quickly. You can also move them around everywhere and it is very easy.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Strawberries/Bananas
  • Water
  • Various Toppings (etc granola, cereal, honey, peanut butter.)
  1. Put all ingredients into the blender
  2. Whip it up and whip it out
  3. Clean it with soap.
